Whims for a Wedding

Berdandy December 29, 2024

When I decided to ask Rachel if she wanted to get married, I wanted something unique and personal for an engagement ring. With the audacity of curiosity, I wandered into a local goldsmith's shop with the intention of asking for some advice on how to explore making the engagement ring myself.

Serendipity was with me that day, because it turned out that Jason had previously taught workshops, but hadn't in several years. He was open to doing some custom lessons about lost-wax casting.

Fast-forward several months, a few dozen lunchtime lessons later, and I had created an engagement ring in my own custom claddagh design
