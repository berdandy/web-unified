Hobbies of hobbies

Berdandy December 23, 2024

It's hobbies, all the way down...

I make games professionally, and most of my hobbies are creative endeavours. And even some of my hobbies have progressed into semi-professional status.

To some degree, I guess my true hobby is collecting hobbies. And then with enough time, they turn into skills and careers.

The goal for this site is to have a home on the internet that can unify all this stuff in a single location, instead of going to This Place for music works, That Place for tabletop design works, The Other Place for video game works, The Fourth Place for physical crafting works, and so on and so on.

It's likely to be a little chaotic, but with a throughline of creativity.

Enjoy.
